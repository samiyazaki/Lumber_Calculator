About Lumber

Lumber is a type of wood that has been processed into beams and planks, typically used for construction purposes.

It is one of the most commonly used materials in the building industry, known for its strength, durability, and versatility.

Lumber is available in various species, grades, and sizes, each with its own unique characteristics and applications.
