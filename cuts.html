Cut Sheet Calculator

Enter the details for each part:

- Item Name: Enter the name of the item.

- Lengths Cut: Enter the lengths you cut for each part, separated by commas.

- Cut Into: Enter what you cut each length into for each part, separated by commas.

Click on the "Add" button to add the part to the calculator.

The calculator will calculate the difference between lengths cut and lengths cut into.

A green checkmark will indicate equal lengths, and a red cross will indicate unequal lengths.

Use the "Export to Excel" button to export the calculator data to an Excel spreadsheet.
