Board Foot Calculator

Enter the details of each wood item in the tally:

- Label: Enter a label or name for the wood item.

- Length (feet): Enter the length of the wood item in feet.

- Number of Pieces: Enter the number of pieces of the wood item.

Click on the "Add" button to add the item to the tally.

The tally will calculate the total length and number of pieces as you add items.

Use the "Export to Excel" button to export the tally data to an Excel spreadsheet.
